Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)

Introduction

Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) are a class of neural networks designed for handling graph-structured data.

They have gained popularity in various domains, including social networks, recommendation systems, and biology.

Key Features

  • Graph Convolutional Layers
  • Node and Graph Classification
  • Message Passing

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

Introduction

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) are a class of deep learning models commonly used in computer vision tasks.

They are known for their ability to capture spatial patterns in data.

Key Features

  • Convolutional Layers
  • Pooling Layers
  • Feature Maps
